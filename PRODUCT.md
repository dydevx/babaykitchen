# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local guests in Braunschweig who want to check the menu, opening hours, location, and phone contact quickly before visiting or ordering for pickup.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Ba Bay Kitchen as a small, authentic Vietnamese restaurant. Success means visitors understand the food, trust the freshness, and can reach the restaurant or navigate there without friction.

## Brand Personality

Warm, handmade, direct. The site should feel flavorful and cared-for without becoming decorative or hard to read.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic restaurant templates, beige craft sameness, heavy stock-photo gloss, fake luxury, and cluttered menu PDFs.

## Design Principles

- Let real food photography carry the appetite.
- Keep ordering information and contact actions obvious.
- Preserve Vietnamese dish names and German explanations clearly.
- Make the menu easy to scan on phones.
- Use motion only as light feedback, not as decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly navigation, readable type sizes, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that do not hide important contact actions.
